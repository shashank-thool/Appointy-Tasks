Delhi CM launches 'Red Light On, Gaadi Off' campaign to curb pollution

Delhi CM Arvind Kejriwal has launched the 'Red Light On, Gaadi Off' campaign to tackle air pollution in the national capital. Kejriwal stated, "There are one crore vehicles registered in Delhi. According to experts, even if 10 lakh vehicles turn off ignition at traffic signals, then 1.5 tonnes of PM10 will reduce in a year."
